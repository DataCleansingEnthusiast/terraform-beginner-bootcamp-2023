Shakti Fusion Band

Shakti Band is a dynamic and energetic music group that has been captivating audiences worldwide with their unique fusion of music and cultural influences.

About Shakti Band

Shakti Band, founded by legendary guitarist John McLaughlin and tabla maestro Zakir Hussain, is known for its ability to seamlessly blend traditional and contemporary sounds, creating a musical experience that transcends boundaries. The band members are not only talented musicians but also passionate about promoting cultural diversity through their art.

Key Members

Shakti Band features the following key members:

  • John McLaughlin: A world-renowned guitarist, John McLaughlin is known for his pioneering work in fusion music. His innovative guitar playing has left an indelible mark on the world of music.
  • Zakir Hussain: Tabla maestro Zakir Hussain is a virtuoso percussionist and composer. He is celebrated for his mastery of the tabla and his ability to bridge musical traditions.
  • L.Shankar: renowned Indian violinist and composer. He is a pioneering figure in the world of fusion music, having played a pivotal role in the group Shakti, which fused Indian classical music with jazz, and his contributions have left a lasting impact on the world of cross-cultural musical collaborations.
  • T.H. Vikku Vinayakram: a highly acclaimed Indian percussionist known for his mastery of the ghatam.

Music Genres

Shakti Band explores a wide range of musical genres, including:

  • Indian Classical Music
  • Rock
  • Fusion
  • World Music
